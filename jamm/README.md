# JAMM (Just Another Magic Mod)
#### Built by Henry Oliver

### Features:
#### Rings
- Ring
- Ring of Aqua (makes you breath underwater)
- Ring of Vita (makes you regen health quickly)
- Ring of Fuga (makes you fly)
- Ring of Festina (makes you go very fast)
